Atlanta

Atlanta Botanical Garden

The Atlanta Botanical Garden has the iconic Orchid Display House and the Canopy Walk, a 600-foot suspension bridge.

Atlanta History Center

Housing the largest collection of Civil War memorabilia in the Southeast, the Atlanta History Center has the acres of gardens and the basketball hardwood from the 1996 Centennial Olympic Games.

Centennial Olympic Park

Centennial Olympic Park, housed in the center of Atlanta's downtown convention and entertainment district, serves as Atlanta's legacy from the 1996 Olympic Games.
