About The Living Price Calculator


This calculator is a tool that can be utulized by the user to plan out future expenses they may have if they choose to live in one of 16 U.S. cities that this tool offers. The cost of living is compared on four metrics: median household income, the average cost of a gallon of gas and the average cost of transportation.
