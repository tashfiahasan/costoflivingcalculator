The Liberty Bell Center

Fun fact: Many historians believe the 44-pound clapper caused the bell to famously crack on its first use. Visitors can tour The Liberty Bell Center for free year-round.

Reading Terminal Market

Reading Terminal Market, the city’s famous indoor food paradise that’s a one-stop shop for local produce, delicious sandwiches, fresh-baked pastries and artisanal cheeses available from dozens of vendors.

Longwood Gardens

Longwood Gardens attracts visitors — more than 1.4 million in 2018 — from around the globe to its 1,100 acres in Kennett Square that are filled with stunning gardens, woodlands, meadows and fountains.
