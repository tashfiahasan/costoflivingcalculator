Houston Zoo

Fun Fact: The zoo houses over 6,000 animals from 900 species. It receives 2.1 million visitors each year and is the second most visited zoo in the United States.

Discovery Green

Fun Fact: A dozen trees, including 4 live oaks, were relocated to Discovery Green before it opened, instead of being destroyed. The live oaks are over 100 years old.
